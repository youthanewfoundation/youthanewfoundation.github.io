About YAFA

Who We Are

YAFA is a small charity determined to empower the ever growing number of streets children with the basic skills and tools they need to face the challenges of life. Whe hunt for these vulnerable kids in our bid to protect them from the dangers of street. Simply put - we try to get to these kids before the street does. We offer street children home, food, medical care, protection, love and a parental care.

Mission

Our mission is to locate and empower as many street children as we can and deliver to them that childhood every child deserves.We basically train and camp street children aged 6 months and above ( and migrants ) to see the light of hope and benefit or usefulness of education and vocation. The street youth are trained in various vocations to help them earn a living. As host family, we give these children the smiles every child needs for good socialization - we become that family these children never had and we value that.
